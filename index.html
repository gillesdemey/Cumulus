Cumulus

A SoundCloud player that lives in your menubar.


Download View on GitHub

Unobtrusive

Music is meant to be listened to — without getting in the way of your daily tasks.

Notifications

Cumulus will notify you when a new track is playing, so you always know what you're listening to.

Media Keys

Cumulus supports media keys out of the box, no hassle and no extra setup required.

Fast

It's also really fast, thanks to the technologies that power it.

Interested? Take a look under the hood.
